The registration fee for each participant is 500 CNY. Please note that payment will only be accepted in cash at the conference venue. Thank you for your understanding and cooperation.

Participants are responsible for their own meals and accommodation expenses. A limited number of rooms are available at the conference venue, including double rooms and twin rooms. If you would like to stay at the conference venue, please provide the required information via the registration link. Due to limited availability, we cannot guarantee that a room will be available for every applicant.
